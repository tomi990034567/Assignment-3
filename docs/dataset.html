The Dataset

Curating Data and Designing Algorithms
Visit GitHub Page

The dataset we are using for this project was agreed upon by the entire team on our first briefing online meeting. One team member provided a large list of books on psychology. After that we doubled-checked its accuracy alongside the team. After eliminating the fiction literature and adapting the list to our established project needs, the 65 books remaining was pictured as the foundation to an Introductory course In Psychology. The dataset is divided into mandatory and recommended literature.

The Role

The role of this dataset is to give an overall view on human being intrinsic development. This is done by providing literature from different areas:

  • Clinical Psychology
  • Clinical Psychiatry
  • Neuroscience
  • Philosophy
  • Religion and Religion History
  • Non-Fiction
